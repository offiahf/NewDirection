Our History

New Direction church was founded in 2009 by Dr James Roberts. After graduating from college, the founder Pastor roberts had a calling from God. The church started off with about 10 to 15 congregation every sunday and grew to hundreds of congregation over the years.New Direction church in know for its charitable work in the neighborhood like feeding the homeless once every week and cleaning up the neighborhood on saturdays. The church aslo hosts online service for people who cannot attend our live service on their facebbok and youtube platform, so feel free to attend our online service or in person.
